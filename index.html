About the Project

The project CollaboVis primarily focuses on understanding the relationship between the growth of research communities and the diversity of topics over time. We have collected from the Scopus database the publications of researchers of all universities in the Philippines from 1960 to 2020, create a weighted and temporal co-authorship network, and identify the communities for different time periods.
